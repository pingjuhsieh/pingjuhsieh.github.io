Welcome!

I am an aspiring economist interested in Industrial Organization and Microeconomic Theory.

Previously, I received a B.A. in Economics, with a minor in Mathematics, from National Taiwan University. Before that I worked as an Actuarial Analysis Intern at PwC and as an Automotive IoT Data Analysis Intern at a Taiwanese car manufacturing company.

Introduction

My research interests include microeconomics, Industry Organization, and decision science. I am particularly interested in questions related to data policy evaluation and household decision-making.

I am currently developing EconLib, a Lean 4 library dedicated to formalizing economic theory. The project aims to provide researchers with rigorous, machine-verified tools that bridge the gap between economic intuition and formal logic. It is a fully open-source initiative, and contributions from both economists and Lean enthusiasts are highly welcome!
